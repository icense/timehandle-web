Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

🔒 Core Commitment: TimeHandle Does Not Collect Any Personal Information
No registration, no login, no server upload. All data stays on your phone.

1. Data Collection

TimeHandle does not collect any personal information. We have no servers and do not transmit any data to third parties.

  • ❌ No collection of personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • ❌ No collection of device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • ❌ No collection of location information
  • ❌ No collection of usage statistics

2. Calendar Data

TimeHandle needs to read your system calendar to provide core functionality. All calendar data:

  • ✅ Stored only locally on your iPhone/iPad
  • ✅ Never uploaded to any server
  • ✅ Never shared with any third party
  • ✅ Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

You can revoke calendar access at any time in iOS system settings. After revocation, the app will no longer be able to read your calendar.

3. Purchase Information

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple StoreKit:

  • Apple handles all payment information; TimeHandle cannot access your payment details
  • The app only verifies your subscription status via StoreKit API and does not store any purchase records
  • Verification occurs locally on your device, without passing through any third-party servers

4. Notification Permission

If you enable notification permission, reminders will be generated locally on your device. All reminder logic executes on-device and involves no data transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

TimeHandle does not use any third-party analytics, statistics, or crash reporting services. No advertising SDKs are integrated.

  • No Google Analytics
  • No Firebase
  • No Facebook SDK
  • No tracking or analytics of any kind

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all data in the following ways:

  • Uninstall TimeHandle: All local data will be immediately cleared
  • Revoke calendar permissions in iOS settings: The app will no longer be able to access your calendar

Since we do not store any server data, no additional deletion request is needed.

7. Children's Privacy

TimeHandle does not collect any user information, including children. All users can use it safely.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes in the app or on the website. Since we do not collect contact information, please check this page periodically.
